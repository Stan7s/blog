J suggested me to talk with our professors who are super nice. This might change your mind on getting along with teachers, she said.

Sadly it won’t. I always know what an ideal teacher-student relationship should be like, and that most of the teachers will not think and behave like those I met in my childhood and adolescense. But it was not untill I placed myself in a normal environment did I realize how much I was tramatized by the past. Knowling what is right and what is not doesn’t mean you can change yourself from inside out. Bad memories hit harder when similar things occured and you are the only one in this room who can‘t breathe.

I feel like I was doing something both extremely eazy and difficult.

跟爸爸妈妈说去看了牙医，被建议拔牙和戴牙套。妈妈说「是我们小时候没照顾好你。」过了一会爸爸也跟我说了一样的话。

每当我成年后跟他们讲自己的不顺心，他们总会反思并且对我说抱歉。好像我没能拥有的东西都是因为他们当年没能给我，而我得到的一切都是因为我自己是一个聪明和勤奋的小孩。

但我只不过是世界上最幸运的小孩。
